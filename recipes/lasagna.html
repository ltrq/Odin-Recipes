How to cook Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a type of pasta dish, typically made of stacked layers of pasta, filled with varying amounts of cheese, sauce, and other ingredients, and baked at a high temperature

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 clove of garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • 1 (15 ounce) can canned tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano, or to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt, or to taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper, or to taste
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded Fontina cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 pound shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 3/4 cup grated Cheddar cheese
  • 3 cups shredded Fontina cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. In a large skillet over medium heat, brown ground beef and onion in a large skillet. Stir in garlic and cook until lightly browned, about 5 minutes.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, and water. Season with oregano, salt, pepper, and remaining ingredients.
  4. Bring to a simmer and simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  6. Spread 1 cup of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over meat sauce.
  7. Spread 1/2 of the ricotta cheese over the noodles.
  8. Spread 1/2 of the egg over the ricotta cheese. Arrange 6 more noodles over the egg. Spread 1/2 of the mozzarella cheese over the noodles.
  9. Spread 1/2 of the cheddar cheese over the mozzarella cheese. Arrange 6 more noodles over the cheddar cheese. Top with 1/2 of the Parmesan cheese.
